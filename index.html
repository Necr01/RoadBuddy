Platform Features

Everything You Need When Your Vehicle Breaks Down

From finding the nearest mechanic to tracking their arrival, Road Buddy provides a complete roadside assistance experience built for Filipino motorists.

Location-Based Matching

Automatically find the nearest verified service providers and auto parts suppliers based on your GPS location using integrated geolocation technology.

Periodic Status Updates

Receive regular updates on your provider's progress with practical periodic location tracking designed for real-world connectivity conditions.

Verified Providers

All service providers undergo admin verification and maintain active subscriptions, ensuring quality control and accountability for every interaction.

In-App Messaging

Communicate directly with your matched provider through built-in messaging — coordinate details, share photos, and stay informed without switching apps.

Auto Parts Sourcing

Find nearby auto parts suppliers alongside mechanics. Get the parts you need quickly with location-filtered results and supplier information.

Ratings & Feedback

Rate service providers after each interaction and read reviews from other motorists — building a trusted community of automotive professionals.

How It Works

Help Is Just Four Steps Away

Whether you have a flat tire, engine trouble, or need emergency towing — getting help is fast and straightforward.

Submit a Request

Open Road Buddy, describe your vehicle issue, and your location is automatically detected via GPS.

Get Matched

Our system instantly finds nearby verified service providers and parts suppliers and notifies them of your request.

Track Progress

Receive periodic status updates on your provider's arrival and communicate via in-app messaging.

Get Back on the Road

Once service is completed, rate your provider and get safely back on the road with confidence.
